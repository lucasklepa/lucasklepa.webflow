Sunrise NFTÂ Trees

Sunrise Stake is a blockchain-based Web3 project designed to offset carbon footprints without depleting your crypto assets. Users can transparently donate their staking rewards, which are converted into virtual trees planted on their island. These trees grow over time, and once they form a fully grown forest, the user can claim it as an NFT.

My role in the project involved creating various tree designs at different growth stages and developing the surrounding environment using generative AI. Sunrise Stake's innovative approach and compelling UI earned it the "Best UI" award at the 2023 Grizzlython, a Solana-hosted hackathon where the idea was first developed.
