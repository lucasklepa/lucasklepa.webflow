Next Level Medical

Next Level Medical is a Learning Experience Platform (LXP) that offers courses for physicians with focus on plastic surgery and cosmetic procedures.

UI/UX Designer: Lucas Klepa
Art Director: Bruno Diza
‍Product Designer: Gustavo Melles
Back-end Developer: Humberto Falcão
Front-end Developer: Henrique Guerra
Agencies: Upper ADucation / Alta Perfomance Web

‍About The Client:
‍Next Level Medical is a leading provider of medical education for plastic surgeons. They offer high budget courses that focus on the latest techniques and innovations in the field, such as breast implants, lipo, rhinoplasty, and more. Their goal is to help plastic surgeons stay up-to-date on the latest advancements in the field and continue to provide the best possible care for their patients.

The Problem:
‍Next Level Medical was facing the challenge of outdated and ineffective course delivery for plastic surgeons. They were looking to develop a video streaming platform that would provide easy access to interactive and high-quality courses, allowing surgeons to learn and retain the latest techniques and innovations in the field at their own convenience. This platform aims to solve the problems of inflexibility, lack of interactivity and inability to review the material in their current method.

Introducing Next Level Medical's new streaming service
A platform that takes the learning experience for plastic surgeons to the next level. This platform is designed to be easy to use and accessible for busy surgeons, providing on-demand access to our high-quality courses.

The streaming service not only provides access to our courses, but also creates a community of plastic surgeons where they can connect with their peers, share their experiences, and collaborate to improve patient outcomes.

Interactive Classes
‍The video feature includes a comment section where surgeons can discuss and provide feedback on the courses. They can rate the videos and access complementary material such as documents and infographics to enhance their learning experience. The feedback provided by the surgeons will help us to improve the quality of our courses and make them more effective in their practice.

Community
‍The community feature on each course is a social platform for plastic surgeons to connect, share and learn from one another. They can post, discuss and share experiences, as well as fill out surveys for feedback. They can also post photos and videos of their work, allowing them to showcase their skills and collaborate with other professionals in the field.

Next Level Medical transforms the way plastic surgeons access education and improve their practice.
