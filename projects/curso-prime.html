About The Client:
Curso Prime is a school that prepares students for the vestibular, an entrance exam for Brazilian universities. Specializing in competitive programs like medicine, Curso Prime aims to provide high-quality education to help students succeed in the exam and enter university of their choice.

The landing page features data that highlights Curso Prime's strengths as a course provider and engaging visuals that help to convey the message. This way, showing what makes Curso Prime the best choice.

The courses page list every product offered by Curso Prime, along with detailed information about each course. The page is designed to be easily updatable, so that Curso Prime can add or remove courses as needed.

The blog feature allows Curso Prime to share articles, news, updates, and educational content with visitors. Users are able to leave comments, share posts on social media, and subscribe to the newsletter. It helps to establish Curso Prime as a trusted source of information.
